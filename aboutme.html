Who is Maaz?

I am a Master of Information student here at Rutgers pursuing a dual concentration in Data Science and IT Management. After undergrad I decided I wanted to further my education (particularly in programming) and this class has provided me with a great foundation! When I'm not working or studying, you can find me at national parks, rock climbing gyms, basketball courts or playing with my cat! When it comes to books, my favorite genre is definitely fantasy (although sci-fi is a close second). I've been reading fantasy novels ever since I read Harry Potter, since then I am enchanted with the world of wizards and warriors. My favorite book of all time is The Hobbit and I hope to visit New Zealand one day to see the Shire in person!

Career

I spent most of my life in central New Jersey. I attended East Brunswick High School(10 minutes away from Rutgers). I went to NJIT for my undergrad to study Fin-Tech. It's over there I learned the practice of data analytics which is still a very interesting subject to me. After graduation I worked at a digital marketing company as a data analyst. Since then, I decided I wanted to learn more about technology, data and programming, which is why I decided to pursue my Masters here at Rutgers! I look forward to learning more (inside and outside the classroom) and am very excited to see where my journey takes me!
