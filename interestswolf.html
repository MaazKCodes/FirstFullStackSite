About
Wolves are social carnivores that can be found throughout the world. The ancestors of the domesticated dog, they are known for their complex social structures and distinctive howls.

The Fierce Gray Wolf

Gray wolves were once a common and important predator in the state of New Jersey. Unfortunately, these beautiful creatures were extirpated from New Jersey in the mid 1800s. Reintroducing these integral canines would help bring balance to the New Jersey ecosystem. If you want to observe wolves in their natural habitats you can visit the Lakota Wolf Preserve in Columbia New Jersey.Visit the Lakota Wolf Preserve website here!
