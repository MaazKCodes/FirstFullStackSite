About
Foxes are small omnivorous mammals found throughout the world. These small canines are known for their elegant appearance and revered for their cunning and adaptability.

The Elusive Red Fox

The red fox is a common (though hidden) omnivorous mammal found throughout the state of New Jersey. They play an important role in the state's ecosystem by regulating populations of small prey and even serving as a food source for larger mammals like bobcats and coyotes.
